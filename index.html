About Tiny Fishing

What is Tiny Fishing?

Tiny Fishing is an exciting fishing game, where you can fish online without a pond or a fishing pole. This game is surely the ideal choice for unwinding. So, you can't miss it!

The main objective of Tiny Fishing is to capture fish by dragging your mouse. Then, don't forget to update your equipment to capture larger and more frequent fish.

How to play Tiny Fishing

With just your desktop computer or mobile device, you may play the Tiny Fishing game and go fishing. Let’s see step by step below:

  • First, let's launch the game by visiting https://tinyfishing76.github.io/
  • Then, hold down your fishing line while you click and move your mouse from left to right. 
  • To catch fish, move your hook around the screen while reeling in your line. As soon as your line touches the water, you will get compensated for every fish you capture.
  • You can make more money as you go deeper because the fish get rarer.

Outstanding features

  • Simple controls: Just click to cast and move the hook.
  • Variety of fish: Dozens of unique fish types with different values.
  • Upgrade system: Spend your coins to improve depth, hook count, and idle earnings.
  • Colorful visuals: Bright and charming art style that appeals to all ages.
  • Endless gameplay: No time limit or end goal—you can keep fishing forever to beat your records.

Tips to catch the most fish in Tiny Fishing

  • Early depth upgrade: Finding higher-value fish is possible when you go deeper.
  • Try to find uncommon fish because some are worth a lot more than others. Acknowledge them and give them priority.
  • Maximize your hook: You will earn more money more quickly if you can catch more fish on each trip.
  • Keep an eye out for bonus fish: Sometimes, fish that are glowing or special can award you with additional benefits.
  • Make the most of downtime by upgrading offline earnings to continue earning coins while you're away.
  • Don't catch low-value fish while your hook is nearly full.
